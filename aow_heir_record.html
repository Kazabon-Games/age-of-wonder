Age of Wonder
A Diceless Tabletop Roleplaying Game

Age of Wonder is an open-world sandbox TTRPG set in the kingdom of Aetheria Nova. There are no dice. Outcomes are determined by the weight of your character's will, the choices they have made, and the consequences those choices set in motion.

Your adventure begins in Shemsara — seat of King Hector, home of the Small Council, where noble heirs come to be educated and transformed. You arrive at thirteen. By eighteen, the crystal will have spoken.

This is your Heir's Record — the official register of the Grand Library of Shemsara. Fill it in during your session zero call with your GM. Every choice here shapes who your character is before the story begins.

A light/dark toggle sits in the top corner of every page — switch freely, your preference is remembered.

  • 01Identity — who you are and your family's standing at court.
  • 02Noble House — your family, their ideals, their shadow, their district in Shemsara.
  • 03Five Years — what you studied from age 13 to 17. Shapes your magic, combat, and politics. Five years in one discipline unlocks a unique Capstone Specialization.
  • 04The Awakening — the crystal reads five years of will and names your school.
  • 05Character Profile — auto-generated summary of your capabilities across all four encounter types.
  • 06Key NPCs — your Councillor is built from what you defined in Identity; you create four more — Ally, Rival, Contact, Wild Card — who matter most before the campaign begins.
  • 07Your Record — post-awakening note, full summary, and save to JSON for your GM.

Section 3 of 7 · Five Years of Study
Five Years in Shemsara
From age 13 to 17 you chose how to spend your time at court. Each year you focused on one area. Those choices shaped your will — and your will determines which school the crystal reveals at your Awakening.
Specialization: Choosing the same aspect in multiple years unlocks deeper sub-choices — fighting styles within a weapon type, philosophical orientations within a school of magic. Depth costs repetition. Breadth costs range.

Choosing the same aspect all five years unlocks a Capstone Specialization — a unique capability no other heir has access to, paired with a leverage cost reflecting the time you had for nothing else.

Section 4 of 7 · The Awakening
The Awakening Ceremony
Age eighteen. The Sanctum. Every heir stands before the crystal on the same day.

The crystal does not assign a school. It reads five years of choices and reveals the school your will has already become. Complete all five years of study to see what the crystal reveals.
School Affinity — How the Crystal Reads You
The highest bar is what the crystal names. House orientation adds inheritance weight — assumptions your family instilled before formal study began.
Every choice across all five years feeds these bars — not just Magic. A year of Combat, Politics, or Lore each lean toward a particular school in ways that aren't always obvious, and the specific fighting style or specialization you choose can shift things further, sometimes against a school you've otherwise been building toward. Watch the bars move as you choose. The crystal is reading the whole pattern, not just the years you spent in study with it directly.
